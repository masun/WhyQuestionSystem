The official sporting colours of University College Dublin are "St. Patrick's Blue and Saffron", adopted in 1910.GAA county colours of County Dublin include light blue jerseys. In the National University of Ireland's academic dress code, "Saint Patrick's Blue" is the colour of the faculty of Science; Veterinary Medicine has a darker "Celtic Blue".Royal College of Surgeons of Ireland also features St Patrick's blue.Trinity College Dublin fencing club specifies that the azure in its colours is "St. Patrick's Blue (Pantone 295 as the Presedential [sic] Pennant)".
