Super Why! or The Reading Adventures of Super Why! is a CGI animated show developed by Angela C. Santomero and Samantha Freeman Alpert. The TV series is produced by New York City-based Out of the Blue Enterprises and Toronto-based DHX Media through its Decode Entertainment division.PBS Kids and PBS Kids Sprout in the USA, on Nick Jr. in the United Kingdom, and Kids' CBC in Canada. Thai PBS from Thailand broadcasts the shortened version (10 minutes), the episodes are 5 years behind the U.S.
